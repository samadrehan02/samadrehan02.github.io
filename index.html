AI Medical Scribe Research Open Source

Real-time transcription and structured clinical note generation from noisy Hindi/Hinglish doctor–patient conversations.

Research focus: Hallucination control, structured generation, and error propagation in low-resource, code-switched medical ASR pipelines.

System: Live audio → ASR → entity extraction → constrained LLM → OPD notes

  • Studied entity-first pipelines vs end-to-end generation
  • Evaluated schema-constrained decoding under noisy ASR
  • Designed session-scoped, auditable workflows

Outcomes: Reduced hallucinated clinical facts, improved structural validity, and identified normalization as a dominant failure source.
